View Testimonials

Fine Finishing is a Downriver based, total construction & restoration company with a team of experts specializing in property restoration resulting from fire, wind & water damage, as well as additions & custom remodeling in your home or business. We handle each and every project with a hands-on, personal approach.


Our clients enjoy the consistency of dealing with the same point people throughout their project. Our long time staff handles each home as if it were their own, taking extra measures for dust control and cleanliness. The quality of our workmanship is second to none!

The Fine Finishing team is very knowledgeable in both construction and building codes. We have two licensed builders and a registered building inspector for the State of Michigan on staff. Along with our construction knowledge, we staff two full time estimators that use Xactimate software to streamline pricing for both the client and the insurance companies.


You will also find a link here, as well as in the corner of our page for our sister company, Access Construction. Access provides accessibility services & home modifications.
