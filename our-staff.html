Our Staff

President Mike Rickman & Vice President Jeff Mitchell:

Business partners since 1993, their building careers started with general construction and has grown into more specialized construction in structural modifications, insurance restoration and handicap accessibility. Jeff and Mike both possess building, building inspecting and plan review licenses. As family men, both are aware of and appreciate the comforts and freedoms that are desired in all aspects of life. Their philosophy is to make the accessible transition as quick and stress-free as possible. This ensures that our clients will be treated with the same compassion that they would want for their own family members. Mike and Jeff assume different roles in the company. Mike specializes in design, with keeping the needs and wants for the clients in mind. Jeff specializes in efficiency, with a focus on executing and managing projects in a timely manner, without sacrificing quality.

Project Manager & Insurance Specialist - Dianna Moore:

Dianna Moore has over 17 years of construction experience, specializing in an array of simple to comprehensive insurance work. She is a liaison between the industry professionals, insurance adjusters and Fine Finishing. Dianna uses Xactimate software for all estimates. This software is recognized by the insurance companies and streamlines the cost-effectiveness for the job.
